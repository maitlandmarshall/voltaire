# Scene 0 — Reality Slip & Capture (Unwinnable Set-Piece)

This scene is the on-ramp from `Adventures/2025-11-22.md` into Voltaire’s Head-Space™ module. It should feel cinematic, scary, and *obviously rigged*—but still reward clever play with concrete carry-forward benefits.

## Start Point (Hard Canon)

- Location: the buried temple under Anauroch, right after Aelrindë reveals herself.
- Last words hanging in the air: **“Do not fear, you will not break, you will bend.”**
- The dark mythallar hums like a throat clearing.

## The Reality Slip (read-aloud-ish beats)

Pick 3–5 and layer them over 1–2 minutes:

- Torchlight desaturates; colors go “draft-gray”.
- You can see **headings** hovering over pillars (e.g., “## WEST HALL”).
- Your own spoken words briefly appear as **subtitles** in the air… then get crossed out.
- Distant quill-scratching grows louder than breathing.
- A thin black “margin” appears along the edge of a wall. It widens.
- Aelrindë’s silhouette “lags” a half-second behind her body like bad animation.

Voltaire (cheerful, clinical, disembodied):
> “Ah. Excellent. We’ve arrived at the *interesting* part.”

Shar (only some PCs hear it; cold, close):
> “Loss is a door. Step through.”

## Fight Structure (how it stays fun)

- **Timebox:** 3–5 rounds. Plan to end on Round 4 unless your table is vibing hard.
- **Win condition:** what they **protect, steal, learn, or prove**, not monster HP.
- **Telegraph:** early, in-world: “You cannot win. You can only choose what you carry forward.”

### What “success” buys (choose 2–4 total, spread across PCs)

Use these as rewards for clever actions, RP, or risk:

- **Keep 1 extra item** through capture (beyond your normal “signature item” rule).
- **Gain +1 Favor** at the start of the Ludus (Scene 1).
- **Steal a Key-Tag** (opens one locker later).
- **Gain a Gate clue** (one Circle/Square/Triangle solution hint).
- **Mark of the Margin:** advantage on one check during intermission (Scene 3).

## End Condition (capture script)

At the end of the chosen final round (usually Round 4):

- The arena “page” hard-cuts: a loud **page flip**.
- Everyone makes a **Wisdom save** (DC in `02 - Environment (Scene 0).md`).
  - Fail: incapacitated until the smash cut.
  - Success: you’re still captured, but you gain one carry-forward reward.

Voltaire:
> “Cut. Wonderful data. Now—let’s workshop you.”
