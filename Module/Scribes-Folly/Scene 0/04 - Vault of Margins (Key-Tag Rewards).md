# Vault of Margins (Key-Tag Rewards)

Key-Tags are more than locker keys: the Quillmaster can “bundle” excess tags into access for higher-tier rewards. This turns Key-Tags into a PvP currency without forcing PvP.

## Rules

- Key-Tags are physical. They can be traded, stolen, wagered, and demanded as “fees.”
- **Vault gear is a stage-prop of Head-Space™**: it functions normally *inside the one-shot*, but can’t leave the mindscape unless you explicitly want it to.
- If you want to keep pacing fast: attunement to Vault gear happens as a montage and doesn’t cost real-time.

## Exchange Rates (recommended)

- **1 Key-Tag:** open 1 locker (recover 1 confiscated item).
- **2 Key-Tags:** open the Vault (Rare) — choose 1.
- **3 Key-Tags:** open the Vault (Legendary) — choose 1.

## Vault (Rare) — 2 Key-Tags (pick one)

- **Torn Page (Major):** a single-use effect approximating a 3rd–5th level spell (you pick; match the moment).
- **Potion Bundle:** potion of speed + potion of greater healing.
- **Sponsor Intervention Token:** once, remove one arena hazard this bout (declare at initiative 20).
- **Ink Salve:** once, as a bonus action, end one condition on yourself (blinded, deafened, frightened, poisoned, restrained).

## Vault (Legendary) — 3 Key-Tags (pick one)

These are intentionally strong, but bounded (1/bout or limited uses).

### 1) The Protagonist’s Cloak (Wondrous, legendary, attunement)

- You gain advantage on initiative and Charisma checks made to perform for the crowd.
- **Once per bout:** when you would drop to 0 HP, you instead drop to 1 HP and the cloak flares with subtitles.

### 2) Quill of Rewrite (Wondrous, legendary, attunement)

- **3 charges.** When a creature you can see within 60 ft makes a d20 roll, you may spend 1 charge to force a reroll (must use the new roll).
- When you use this, a faint **margin note** appears on your skin describing what you changed.

### 3) Shar’s Forgetting Veil (Wondrous, legendary, attunement)

- **Once total:** as a bonus action, you create magical darkness in a 15-ft radius centered on you for 1 minute. You can see through it.
- When the veil ends, choose: lose a minor personal memory for the rest of the one-shot **or** take 2d6 psychic damage (your choice, no save).

### 4) Glasya’s Exit Clause (Wondrous, legendary, attunement)

- **Once per bout:** when you are hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to teleport up to 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see and become invisible until the end of your next turn.
- After you use it, you owe Glasya’s Advocate a “signature” (a future hook).

### 5) Beshaba’s Loaded Die (Wondrous, legendary, attunement)

- **Once per bout:** after you roll a d20, you may treat it as a **natural 20**.
- The DM gains one **Misfortune** token. At any later dramatic moment, the DM may spend it to treat one of your d20 rolls as a **natural 1** (announce it as Beshaba laughing).

### 6) Marginblade (Weapon, legendary, attunement)

- A +2 finesse weapon of your choice (dagger/shortsword/scimitar).
- **Once per turn** when you hit, deal +2d6 psychic damage.
- **Once per bout** when you score a critical hit, the target must succeed on a Wisdom save (DC = 8 + PB + your Dex/Str mod) or be frightened of you until the end of its next turn.
