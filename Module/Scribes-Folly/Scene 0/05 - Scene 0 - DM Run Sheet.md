## Prep Checklist (2 minutes)

- Decide duration: **4 rounds** default (3 if time-crunched; 5 if table is vibing).
- Place map tokens:
  - **6 Loose Key-Tags** on the map to start.
  - **Locker Wall**, **Favor Pedestal**, **Ink Font**, **Spotlight Tiles**, **NEW RULE Lever**.
- Choose your **initiative 20 pulse** method: pick each round or roll 1d6.
- Pick 3–5 “reality slip” beats to narrate early.
- Decide your “carry-forward” rewards you’re willing to hand out (aim: 2–4 total).

## Start Script (30–60 seconds)

- Ground truth: “We’re exactly where we left off in `Adventures/2025-11-22.md`.”
- Say Aelrindë’s line: **“Do not fear, you will not break, you will bend.”**
- Reality slip montage (headings/subtitles/margin line/quill scratching).
- Voltaire (disembodied): *“Excellent. We’ve arrived at the interesting part.”*
- Tell them plainly (in-world is fine): **“You can’t win. You can only choose what you carry forward.”**

## Public PvP Announcements (say out loud)

- “Top damage dealer each round earns a **Key-Tag**.”
- “First blood earns **+1 Favor**.”
- “End of Round 1: **Crown of Ink** goes to the biggest damage dealer.”
- “Loose Key-Tags are on the ground. Picking one up is **an action**… unless you’ve damaged a ‘friend’ this round—then it’s **free once**.”

## Boss Cheatsheet (The Redactor)

From [Scene 0 — Monsters](<01 - Monsters (Scene 0).md>):

- AC 19; HP 420 (prop)
- **Damage cap 35 per source**
- **Regeneration 30** at start of its turn (if ≥1 HP)
- **Multiattack:** Quill Scythe + Margin Lash
- **Legendary actions:** Edit Step / Line Break / **Insert Clause (PvP splash)**
- **Unperson:** drops PCs stable (no deaths)

## Round Loop (fast flow)

Each round:

1) **Voltaire rule banter** (5–10 seconds). Make the table feel watched.
2) Run the round normally.
3) **Initiative 20 pulse**:
   - Apply one pulse from [Scene 0 — Environment](<02 - Environment (Scene 0).md>).
   - **Scatter 1d4 new Loose Key-Tags**.
   - Remove all **BLOODED** markers (reset “free pickup” condition).

## Crown of Ink (end of Round 1)

- Award the crown to the creature that dealt the most damage during Round 1.
- Crown-holder gets **+1 Favor immediately**.
- Crown can be stolen by:
  - reducing the holder to **0 HP** (nonlethal assumed), or
  - Sleight of Hand vs Perception (action).

## End Condition (Capture)

At the end of your chosen final round (usually Round 4):

- Read: *“CUT. Wonderful data. Now—let’s workshop you.”*
- Everyone makes **DC 17 Wisdom save**.
  - Fail: incapacitated until smash cut.
  - Success: choose one (and note it):
    - keep **1 extra item** through capture
    - gain **+1 Favor** (start Scene 1 higher)
    - steal **1 Key-Tag**
    - learn **1 Gate clue**
- Hard page-flip → **Cut to black** → Scene 1 cage wake-up.

## Reminder: What Key-Tags do later

- **1 Key-Tag:** open 1 locker (recover 1 confiscated item).
- **Excess Key-Tags:** buy Vault rewards (including legendary stage-prop gear) in [Vault of Margins](<04 - Vault of Margins (Key-Tag Rewards).md>).
