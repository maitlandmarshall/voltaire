# Canon Primer (Voltaire Vault)

This module is designed to be *canon-friendly* with the rest of this Obsidian vault. The goal is not to retell the campaign, but to keep the tone, names, and metaphysics consistent when the one-shot happens “inside [[Voltaire]]’s mind”.

## Core Premise

- [[Voltaire]] has finished his living crab-book-tail and formally titled it **[[Machinations & Actions - Player's Handbook]]**.
- [[Shar]] (Lady of Loss) declined to “play”, but **agreed to facilitate invitations/attendance** for Voltaire’s Head-Space™ game/trial.
- [[Glasya]] is on the invite list and is likely present (in some form) as a patron, observer, or contract-offerer.
- **Start point**: the one-shot begins immediately after `Adventures/2025-11-22.md` in the buried temple under Anauroch (dark mythallar; druid Aelrindë; god-golems).

## Voltaire (Tone & Constraints)

- **High INT / Low WIS**: intricate logic, catastrophically bad judgment.
- **Body horror**: unblinking eyes, fixed smile (frowning hurts), tattoos, donkey tail merged with the crab-book.
- **Meta obsession**: reality-as-rules, “tests”, “data”, “narrative yield”, abrupt “new rules”.
- **Sun Card**: a 6d8 radiant blast exists in-canon as a homebrew boon from the Deck.

## Divine / Cosmic Stakes

- [[Shar]]: darkness, loss, secrets, forgetfulness; deeply tied to the party (Cornholio, Cromash, Yennefer).
- [[Beshaba]]: misfortune; thematically relevant to Voltaire’s life and “luck-as-cosmic-pressure”.
- [[Glasya]]: contracts, politics, structured sin; a natural “sponsor” for a gladiatorial escape.

## Party Touchstones (Use as Echoes/Patrons/Enemies)

- [[Cornholio]]: Echo Knight; Shar’s consort; wields **[[Shadow's Weave]]**.
- [[Cromash]]: Paladin of Grûumsh; “donates” to Shar via a soul-sending weapon; Skull Museum in [[Palischuk]].
- [[Dagoth]]: chronicler, ritualist; obsessive about systems and identification.
- [[Yennefer]]: grafted wings; whip tied to Shar; volatile wildcard.
- [[Tamerac]]: gate-to-Avernus eye socket; discordant chaos.
- [[Elora]]: archer; prior arena fighting (Abeil); prophetic receptiveness.
- Sharias (blue tiefling; Shar devotee; rivalry with Yennefer) is an easy “rival gladiator” face to pull in.

## Key Artifact / Power (Great for Puzzles)

- [[The Ink of Unbeing]]: extract memories → inscribe into the book → tattoo onto others.
  - **Blessing of Forgetfulness** vs **Curse of Eternal Recall** is a perfect Shar-flavored “choice gate”.
