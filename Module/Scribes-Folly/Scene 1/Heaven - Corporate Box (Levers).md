# Heaven Box — Corporate Levers (Tina)

Quick-reference for Tina’s **Heaven corporate box** controls.

---

## Lever 63 (delayed consequence ideas)

Tina pulled the same lever again (roll **63**) and nothing happened immediately. Next turn, pick one “bad news” payoff:

1) **Control Slip (Recommended).** DC 15 Int (Arcana) to keep control; on fail, Elder Brain acts on init 20 and targets the creature with the highest Favor for 1 round.  
2) **Stuart Lever Fires.** Stuart’s free-will clause tightens: for 1 round he must approach Tina’s chosen target and make a nonlethal attack (or lose 2 Favor if he refuses).  
3) **Rose-Thorn Hazard.** At next init 20, make two 10-ft thorn patches (difficult terrain); first entrant takes 2d6 psychic.  
4) **Sponsor Audit.** Glasya invoice: pay 1 Favor or Tina rewrites 1 rule for 1 round (“no reactions” / “no bonus actions” / “no healing”).

**Immediate fire option:** **Elder Brain Echo** returns at **1 HP**, slips control for 1 round, uses Mind Break, then collapses leaving a “## REVENGE” heading over Tom.

## Stuart’s lever (linked) — d100 (66% screw him / 33% boon)

Roll `1d100`:

- `01–66` = **FUCK STUART UP** (roll `1d8`)  
- `67–99` = **MASSIVE BOON** (roll `1d8`)  
- `100` = **Choice/Misprint** (Tina chooses screw-up or boon, then roll `1d6` side-effect)

### 01–66 (bad) `1d8`

1) Puppet String (Tina controls his move + action 1 turn)  
2) Free-Will Invoice (lose 5 Favor or charmed by Tina 1 turn)  
3) Armor Redaction (AC -2 until init 20)  
4) Vow Feedback (Vow off 1 round + 2d6 psychic)  
5) Giant Belt Slip (STR dmg -3 until end next turn)  
6) Spotlight Shame (attacks vs Stu adv this round)  
7) Ink Hemorrhage (if Stu deals dmg next turn, takes 1d10 necrotic)  
8) Drop Cage (Str save DC 15 or yanked 20 ft + prone/restrained)

### 67–99 (boon) `1d8`

1) Autonomy Surge (free-will clause suspended 1 min/rest of bout)  
2) Champion’s Refill (25 temp HP + end charm/fear)  
3) Overclock (extra action: Attack(1)/Dash/Disengage/Use Object)  
4) Radiant Encore (+4d8 radiant on next hit)  
5) Perfect Vow (Vow on 2 targets for 1 turn)  
6) Sponsor Shield (reduce first damage by 20 this round)  
7) Crowd Favorite (+3 Favor; 1d4 Key-Tags on next hit)  
8) Unkillable Beat (once: drop to 1 HP instead of 0)

### 100 misprint `1d6`

1) Swap labels (next pull affects Tom)  
2) Double-pull (resolve twice; Stu +10 Infamy either way)  
3) Audience static (Con DC 15 or deafened 1 round within 30 ft)  
4) Glasya’s wink (Tina +2 Favor; Stu gains “## OWNED” heading)  
5) Selûne interference (nonlethal-only consequences)  
6) Editor’s note (pay 1 Favor or it targets Tina)

## Selûne boon (rightmost lever 72) — Moonlit Clause + rider

**Moonlit Clause (once/bout):** bonus action choose **Mercy** or **Judgment**.

- **Mercy:** up to 2 creatures gain 15 temp HP and end charm/fear; Tina gains +1 Favor.  
- **Judgment:** 10-ft radius moonlight zone for 2 rounds; enemies start in zone take 2d8 radiant (Con DC 15 half); allies entering gain advantage on next attack (1/round).

**Rider roll** `1d100` (phase stamp). Tina rolled **94 → Blood Moon (Misprint)**: Moonlit Clause **twice** this bout, but after each use **DC 15 Wis** or **2d6 psychic**.
