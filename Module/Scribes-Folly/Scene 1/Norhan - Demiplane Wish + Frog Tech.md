## Wish result (rolled 69) — Demiplane Shift (innate)

Norhan permanently gains an innate **bonus action** to shift herself + chosen **willing allies** into her personal demiplane (hidden from scrying/deities). They can later exit to the exact spot left or nearest safe unoccupied space.

**Time-slip clause (69):** each time the ability is used, roll `1d6`; on **1–2**, the return happens **1 round later** than intended (initiative skip).
