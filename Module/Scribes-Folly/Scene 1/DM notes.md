## Team grouping (dice-close method) + spend-to-bend

### Baseline grouping (fast)

1) Everyone rolls **1d20**.  
2) Sort results.  
3) Pair/group by adjacency (closest together). With 5 players: make **two pairs + one solo** (the solo becomes a “captain” or gets an NPC).

### After they’ve spent on items: reveal **team spend**

Let players spend **points or Favor** to manipulate teams *after* they see the grouping:

- **2 points:** swap positions with an adjacent roll (reforms pairs/groups).
- **3 points:** pull a named NPC (see below) onto your side for this bout.
- **2 Favor:** the crowd “votes” to force **one rivalry pairing** (e.g., Stu and Zeppo must start adjacent / spotlighted).
- **4 Favor:** sponsor intervention: swap one person between two groups (“for drama”).

## Zeppo’s Ashes mechanics (scatter vs throw)

Stu has **~500 gp** of Zeppo’s ashes. Treat this as **5 pinches/uses** (each use consumes a visible puff).

### A) Scatter the ashes (bonus action) → roll 1d8

The ash becomes “stage dust” and the crowd reacts. Roll **1d8**:

1. **Applause Exchange.** Convert **1 Favor → 2 Key-Tags** *or* **2 Key-Tags → 1 Favor** (crowd throws/cheers).
2. **Grudge Spotlight.** Choose two creatures you can see; until next initiative 20, they each have disadvantage to attack anyone **except** each other.
3. **Margin Smoke.** 15-ft radius heavily obscured until next initiative 20.
4. **Quasit Echo.** A 1-HP “Zeppo echo” appears adjacent to a target you choose; the next attack that would hit the target instead hits the echo (then it dissolves).
5. **Contract Dust.** One creature you can see must succeed Wis save (DC 15) or **can’t take reactions** until the start of its next turn.
6. **Tag Shower.** The crowd throws **1d4 Loose Key-Tags** onto random empty squares near the loudest combatants.
7. **Redline.** The next hit you land before end of your turn deals **+2d6 psychic**; you take **1d6 psychic** (Zeppo’s “claim” bites back).
8. **Lucky Draft.** Reroll one d20 you just rolled (must use the new result).

### B) Throw the ashes onto someone (action) → “Ash-Tagged”

Make a ranged attack (improvised, 20/60) vs AC (or Dex save DC 15 if you prefer). On a hit/fail, the target is **Ash-Tagged** until end of bout:
