# Arena Item — Zeppo’s Ashes (“Applause Engine”)

**Quantity:** ~500 gp worth (about **5 pinches/uses**).

---

## A) Scatter the ashes (bonus action) — roll `1d8`

1) **Applause Exchange.** Convert **1 Favor → 2 Key-Tags** or **2 Key-Tags → 1 Favor**.  
2) **Grudge Spotlight.** Choose two creatures; until next init 20 they have disadvantage to attack anyone except each other.  
3) **Margin Smoke.** 15-ft radius heavily obscured until next init 20.  
4) **Quasit Echo.** A 1-HP “Zeppo echo” appears adjacent to a target; next hit that would hit the target hits the echo instead.  
5) **Contract Dust.** One creature Wis save DC 15 or **can’t take reactions** until start of its next turn.  
6) **Tag Shower.** Crowd throws **1d4 Loose Key-Tags** near the loudest combatants.  
7) **Redline.** Next hit you land this turn deals **+2d6 psychic**; you take **1d6 psychic**.  
8) **Lucky Draft.** Reroll one d20 you just rolled (must use the new result).

---

## B) Throw ashes onto someone (action) — “Ash-Tagged”

Ranged improvised (20/60) vs AC (or Dex save DC 15). On hit/fail, target is **Ash-Tagged** until end of bout:

- Can’t benefit from invisibility/hidden (visible ash heading follows them).
- **Once per round**, first time target takes damage, attacker gains **+1 Favor**.
- If target is reduced to **0 HP** (nonlethal), ash pops and drops **1d4 Loose Key-Tags** adjacent.
