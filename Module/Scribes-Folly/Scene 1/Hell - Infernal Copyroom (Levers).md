# Hell Stair — The Infernal Copyroom (Norhan)

**Use:** when Norhan descends the stairwell labeled **HELL** in the ludus.

---

## Set dressing (fast)

- A brick-vault under the arena where **ink boils** like tar and **type blocks** hang on chains.
- The walls are stacked with **misprinted pages** (faces redacted; names crossed out).
- A clerk-devil made of staples (“**The Collator**”) offers: “Pull a lever. Pay a price. Get a result.”

---

## Controls: 5 levers (Hell has levers too)

Each lever pull costs **1 Favor** (or **1 Key-Tag** if Favor is 0). Then roll `1d100`:

- `01–66` = **punishment** (Hell always collects)  
- `67–99` = **boon** (but branded)  
- `100` = **misprint** (choose boon or punishment, then add a twist)

### Lever labels (pick one per pull)

1) **Redline Pain** (damage, fear, stun toggles)  
2) **Contract Clause** (charm/compel/free-will edits)  
3) **Ink Furnace** (hazards, ash/visibility, terrain)  
4) **Name Redaction** (memory, hiding, targeting, “can’t say X”)  
5) **Copy & Paste** (duplicates, echoes, swaps)

---

## `01–66` punishment (roll `1d8`)

1) **Debt Stamp:** Norhan gains **## OWES**; next time she gains Favor, she gains **half** (round down) until end of bout.  
2) **Frog Court Fine:** the frog-bag “tenant” demands payment → lose **20 frogs** (or take **2d6 psychic** if she refuses).  
3) **Heat Seep:** Norhan takes **3d6 fire** (no save) as the furnace breathes; the room becomes lightly obscured by smoke for 1 round.  
4) **Clause Gag:** Norhan can’t speak **one chosen name** (Voltaire/Tina/Stu/Matt) for 1 minute; if she tries, she takes **1d10 psychic**.  
5) **Ink Shackles:** Norhan is **restrained** until end of her next turn (Str save DC 15 to break early).  
6) **Reverse Lever:** the chosen effect targets **an ally/willing creature** instead (Hell “helpfully” redirects).  
7) **Soul Audit:** lose **1 random consumable** (scroll/potion/vial) as it’s “repossessed.”  
8) **Editor’s Spite:** an **Editorial Enforcer** manifests at 1 HP and makes one attack, then dissolves.

---

## `67–99` boon (roll `1d8`)

1) **Hell’s Door Key:** gain **1 Hell Key-Tag** (opens 1 locked thing in the ludus; counts as **2 Key-Tags** for Vault later).  
2) **Clause Break:** once, Norhan can **ignore one imposed rule** (no-air, stunned, charm, etc.) for 1 round.  
3) **Ash Immunity:** Norhan ignores ash difficult terrain/visibility penalties for 1 minute.  
4) **Copy Cut:** teleport up to **30 ft** to a safe space you can see (bonus action, 1 use).  
5) **Infernal Spotlight:** choose one creature; attacks against it gain **+1 to hit** for 1 round and the attacker gains **+1 Favor** the first time they hit it this round.  
6) **Frog Swarm Upgrade:** Frog Swarm duration **+1 round** and damage becomes **3d6** (this bout only).  
7) **Black Clause Mini:** once, Norhan can declare one minor scene fact (DM approves) like a **mini Black Clause**.  
8) **Clone Cleanse:** the bile-covered Clone scroll is “re-inked”; remove one weirdness clause from it (or guarantee it works normally once).

---

## `100` misprint twist (roll `1d6`)

1) The Collator laughs: the lever pull is **free** (no cost) and also grants **+2 Favor**.  
2) The lever triggers in the arena too: Tina’s box lever of the same “type” **fires** next initiative 20.  
3) Hell refunds the price… as **Infamy** (+20 Infamy to a random loud PC).  
4) The room swaps with Heaven for 1 round: Norhan sees the corporate box from below and can pull **one Heaven lever** instead.  
5) A “Voltaire prisoner” echo steps out and offers one sentence of advice, then vanishes.  
6) The next time Norhan uses her demiplane exit, her **Time-slip** is forced (return 1 round late).
