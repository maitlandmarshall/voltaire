# Whip of Flagellation

#item #weapon #shar #yen

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

A magical whip gifted to [[Yennefer]] at a restored shrine to [[Shar]]. It is explicitly framed as a tool for “self-flagellation,” and its blood-shedding property routes blood to the [[Shadowfell]].

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The whip is **+2** (as recorded).
- On hit, the target’s shed blood is sent to the Shadowfell.

## Open Questions

- Is the whip a simple weapon, or a Sharran surveillance conduit?
- Does it create obligations/attention when used repeatedly?
