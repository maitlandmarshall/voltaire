# Carrion Crawler Brain Juice

#item #hazard #alchemy

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

A fine mist trap collected from an elven-style bone puzzle box in tunnels beneath [[Palischuk]]. Dagoth identified the gas as “Carrion Crawler Brain Juice” and stored multiple flasks for later use/research.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The mist hung in the air around the box; a fire bolt passed harmlessly through it.
- Dagoth used `mage hand` to collect samples into vials and used alchemy tools to identify it.
- Approximately **3 flasks** worth were collected (as recorded).

## Open Questions

- Is this a true carrion crawler derivative, or a local/planar analogue?
- What is the mechanical effect when inhaled/applied (paralysis, stun, charm)?
- Who built the trap, and why was it guarding the [[Harper Puzzle Box]] contents?
