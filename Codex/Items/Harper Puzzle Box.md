# Harper Puzzle Box

#item #harpers #puzzle #cache

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

An elven-style bone box found in tunnels beneath [[Palischuk]], associated with a subtle trap mist (see [[Carrion Crawler Brain Juice]]). A Harper later identified a tiny Harper symbol on the box and stated such puzzle boxes are tests given to potential Harpers.

## Known Contents (as recorded)

- 3 uncut star rubies (≈ 1,000 gp each; fist-sized)
- A “curious golden tiara” later identified as [[Hydra's Veil]] and transformed into [[Shadow's Weave]]
- Moonstone gravel and a divine scroll were found nearby (not necessarily inside the box; **[To verify]** exact positioning)

## Notes

- The party initially missed the Harper symbol (later pointed out; a note flags this as potential “retcon”).
