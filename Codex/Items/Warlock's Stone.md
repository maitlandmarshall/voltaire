# Warlock's Stone

#item #communication #teleportation #warlock-knights

![Warlock's Stone](./Warlocks_Stone_ref.png)

## Summary

A paired stone granted by the [[Warlock Knights of Vaasa]] after the party presented evidence of outsider-tainted threats. It functions as a limited sending device and (more ominously) a **teleportation anchor**.

## Known Properties (in-play)

- Comes as a **pair**.
- **Sending**: 2/day.
- **Permanent mending effect** (as recorded in notes).
- **Teleportation anchor**.

## Open Questions

- An anchor for *who*? The bearer, the Knights, a tower ward network, or something else?
- Is the “mending” effect a convenience enchantment, or a surveillance/claim mechanism (always repairing the link)?
