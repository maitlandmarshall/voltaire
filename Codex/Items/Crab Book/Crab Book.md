# Crab Book

#item #crab-book

![Import motif](../../Imports/Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

Voltaire’s horrific, semi-sentient crab-book-tail: a book of ritual spellcasting imbued with a crab’s soul and bound in layered skins. It functions as both spellbook and companion.

## Party Knowledge

- Voltaire’s “book” is physically merged into him (tail graft) and is more than an object.
- The book consumes skin and gains memories (or lingering impressions) from it.
