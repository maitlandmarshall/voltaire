# Magical Spyglass

#item #divination #scouting

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

Cornholio’s magical spyglass is a scouting tool that reveals “glows and dead zones” in the environment—interpreted in notes as **faezress**, **wild magic**, and **null/anti-magic** regions.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- Cornholio used the spyglass while riding the carriage roof during travel.
- The party discussed “magical glows and dead zones visible with his spyglass.”
- Dagoth found the implications intriguing (it shaped their travel vigilance and paranoia).

## Interpretation (inferred; to verify)

- The spyglass may be a layered detection item: revealing magical radiation (faezress), instability (wild magic), and absence (null zones).

## Open Questions

- What is the item’s true name and limitation (range, line-of-sight, false positives)?
- Does it reveal *divine* influence, or only arcane/planar anomalies?
