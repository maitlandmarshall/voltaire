## Summary

A plane of shadow, loss, and dim reflection of the Material—strongly associated with [[Shar]]. It is reachable from the Anauroch temple via the B4 gateway and has hosted Voltaire’s proselytizing under the aspen tree.

## Known Access Points

- [[Anauroch Triumvirate Temple - Mythallar Complex|Anauroch Triumvirate Temple — Mythallar Complex]]: B4 gateway (aspen visible on the Shadowfell side).

## Party Observations (tunnel shrine sequence; to verify exact subregion)

- The party was transported to a “very grey dull looking” plane where **Shadar-kai** (noted as “Netherese?”) were chanting to Shar.
- Cromash was recognized by Sharran devotees like a celebrity, attributed to his history of sending offerings/kills to Shar’s realm.
- In this sequence, Shar bestowed “gifts” (including the [[Whip of Flagellation]] and Shadowfell-flora perfume) and elevated Cornholio’s status via artifacts (see [[Shadow's Weave]] and [[Shadow's Fell]]).
