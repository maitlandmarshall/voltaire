## Summary

A suspected “Circle of Dreams” location identified by the presence of an enormous, pristine statue of [[Titania]] encountered at/near a gate (**2026-01-25**).

## What We Saw (Confirmed)

- An extremely large statue of Titania in great condition.

## Faction Intent (Confirmed)

- The druids’ goal is to **share the ethereal attributes of Titania’s statue** with the wider “wonderful” fae world.

## What We Think It Means (Inferred)

- The statue’s pristine state suggests the area may be protected, curated, or “maintained” like a dreamspace.
- Robin suggests the Circle of Dreams area **resembles the Prime Material Plane**, while its counterpart in the Faerealm is **much grander**—and that the Faerealm version is abandoned but **less desolate**, with a **cyclical** nature (people may return).
