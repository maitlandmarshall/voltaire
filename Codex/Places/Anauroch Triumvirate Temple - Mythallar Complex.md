# Anauroch Triumvirate Temple — Mythallar Complex

## Summary

An ancient underground temple complex beneath the Anauroch desert, dedicated to a triumvirate of **[[Chauntea]]**, **[[Shar]]**, and **[[Eilistraee]]**. The complex is suffused by a **“dark”/umbral-tinged Mythallar** whose influence appears to span the entire site (including multiple basement levels). The temple has been observed *restoring/reconfiguring itself* after major events, seemingly reflecting shifting divine/planar influence.

## Party Knowledge

### Location / Setting
- Beneath the Anauroch desert (Faerûn).
- Architecture: moonstone walls; abundant hanging plants (ivy, night shades, night roses, night orchids, etc.).

### The Mythallar
- Visual: typically sun-like orange/yellow, but here with a subtle umbral hue; described as **“dark”** in party notes.
- Has **three rotating rings** (120° separation) inscribed with Netherese text; believed to define the Mythallar’s area-of-effect and/or governance.
- Observed behavior: “resets” at least some magical effects/runes (noted in earlier sessions).
- “Changed again” after later events (exact mechanism unknown).

### Planar / Divine Interference
- Temple spaces have **reformed/restored** during downtime, with new areas appearing.
- A “strange harmony” was observed between **[[Chauntea]]**, **[[Shar]]**, and **[[Eilistraee]]** after the druid confrontation (see session notes).

### Key Features by Depth (as currently known)

#### Mythallar Dome (core chamber)
- Mosque-like dome room containing the Mythallar.

#### B4 — Shadowfell Gateway
- A 15 ft tall aperture/gateway to **the Shadowfell**.
- A large aspen tree (~100 ft) is visible just beyond; the tree is on the **Shadowfell side** and appears heavily affected by Shadowfell/Shadow Weave energies.
- Shadow essence observed on a collected leaf sample **diminishes on the Material Plane**.
- The party later found Voltaire sitting under the tree, preaching to anthropomorphic shadows and proselytizing that Voltaire is a god (Voltaire only entered the gateway alongside [[Cornholio]]).
- Scene panel (wide): ![Shadowfell aspen meditation (wide)](../../Adventures/2026-01-25/2026-01-25_shadowfell-aspen-meditation_wide.png)
- Later, during a period of quiet proselytizing/meditation under the aspen, Voltaire attempted to “interpret the wind as language,” hearing it as calm blissful humming and humming back in resonance.
- In Voltaire’s presence, lesser shadows melted into the ground; from those spots, flowers grew in the form of ~8 ft tall sunflowers, but a deep umbral purple.
- Three “lesser shades” drifted up into the aspen’s branches and vanished from sight.

![Umbral sunflowers suffused with shadowweave](../../Adventures/2026-01-25/umbral-sunflowers-shadowweave.png)

#### B4 — Rose Garden & Chauntea Hive Room
- A ~40 ft x 40 ft rose garden (red/white/yellow).
- A ~15 ft tall beehive shrine dedicated to **[[Chauntea]]**.
- Builders described as ~1 inch tall bee-like fae sprites wearing garlands and carrying spears.
- A false, fixed “afternoon sky” is visible on the ceiling above this room.

#### Lower Levels (deepest explored)
- A large dirt/field area where holly trees once stood; now sprouts/buds push through ash-black soil.
- Multiple wings that were/are **Mystral/Mystr(a)l archives** were found empty of books.
- A **teleportation circle** was found (exact sigil sequence not recorded here yet).

### Inhabitants / Creatures
- Animated statues/golems of (as recorded) Corellon, Eilistraee, Chauntea, and Mystral (naming confusion noted in party notes).
- “Dark Creepers” working the ash-black soil (described with garlands and farming tools); separately, **outsider-tainted rock gnome corpses** were reanimated and announced they were there to work Chauntea’s grove “in Shar’s name.”
- Local fauna has appeared within the party’s carriage after a Mythallar change (species not enumerated).

### Known Exits / Egress Options (as discussed by the party)
- Teleportation circle (long-term preferred; party currently lacks the spell).
- Shadowfell gateway.
- Plane shift scroll.
- Divine intervention.
