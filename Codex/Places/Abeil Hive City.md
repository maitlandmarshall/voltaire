# Abeil Hive City

#place #city #underdark-adjacent #abeils

![Import motif](../Lore/Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

The Abeil Hive City is a vast subterranean metropolis of the **[[Abeils]]**—a fey, bee-like people—built inside an enormous cavern system that reads (in notes) like the remnants of an ancient underwater sea. The city’s defining “weather” is a constant hum; its defining *engineering* is a colossal flint structure that functions as climate control.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The city is enormous, disciplined, and militarized—large enough to worry surface powers once its existence becomes widely known.
- The city contains a library with access controls/certifications; the party spent days earning entry and studying.
- The queen has near-divine presence/authority (per party characterization in notes).
- There is internal conflict: a heretical Abeil faction in older tunnels worships **[[Dagon]] / [[Mother Hydra]]** (see [[Implements of Mother Hydra]] and [[Outsider Gold]]).

## Observations (from notes; mostly Dagoth’s lens)

- **Cavern geography**: described as an “old underwater sea” cavern system.
- **Roof light source**: a large, city-scale light source mounted/embedded above.
- **Flint “air con” megastructure**: a ~2,000 ft wide / ~1,500 ft high flint structure described as air conditioning.
- **Giant flower forest**: a “forest of giant flowers” surrounding the flint structure.
- **Ambient hum**: familiar over a week; later described as changing to a “hissing cats” vibe near moments of tension/escalation (**[To verify]** whether this was environmental, magical, or social cueing).

## Notable Week-1 Activities (chronicle fragments)

- **Certifications**: mine safety, arcanist, appraisal (Cornholio/Shrek); library access (Dagoth); “hum school” (Tamrenac).
- **Arena bouts**: Elora fought Abeil guards in impromptu fights.
- **Crafting/forges**: Abeil smiths casting a silver/mithral alloy described as ~80/20; arcanists enchanting polearms using masterwork bases.
- **Rewards**: grafted wings surgery for [[Yennefer]]; a modified flying carpet for [[Dagoth]]; [[Horseshoes of the Zephyr]] and gems for [[Cornholio]].
