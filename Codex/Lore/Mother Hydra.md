## Summary

“Mother Hydra” is described (in notes) as an ancient outsider evil deity whose worshippers/agents were embedded in older tunnels beneath [[Palischuk]] and in the mining complex associated with the [[Abeil Hive City]]. Her influence is materially expressed through **[[Outsider Gold]]** arms/armor and the artifact set called the **[[Implements of Mother Hydra]]**.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- A heretic Abeil priestess wielded/guarded Hydra-aligned artifacts.
- Hydra implements appear to:
  - enable warlock-style patronage,
  - store spells,
  - and weaponize divination with “attention” costs (see [[Relic of Augury]]).
