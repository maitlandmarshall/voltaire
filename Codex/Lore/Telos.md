## Summary

Telos is a god referenced in Warlock Knights archive material as **“Master of the Iron Sky.”** In the same source, the Warlock Knights are described as mining a vast **metal god-body** beneath Palashaey/Palischuk.

**Correction (2026-02-21)**: earlier notes wrote “Talos,” but the correct name at this table is **Telos** — a **different god**. When older notes say “Talos” in the context of “Master of the Iron Sky” and the buried metal body, read it as **Telos**.
