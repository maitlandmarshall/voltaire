## Note

**Talos is not Telos.**  

- The buried metal god referenced by the Warlock Knights in this campaign arc is **[[Telos]]** (“Master of the Iron Sky”).
- If “Talos” appears in older notes in *that* context, treat it as a naming mistake and read it as **Telos**.
