# Lunar Silver

#lore #material #shar #transmutation

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

“Lunar silver” is the name given (in notes) to a silver/mithral alloy produced when [[Shar]] transformed an outsider-gold tiara into **[[Shadow's Weave]]** within a restored shrine.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The tiara’s **outsider gold** was transmuted into a silver/mithral alloy described as lunar silver.
- The central ruby was transformed into moonstone in the same moment.

## Interpretation (inferred; to verify)

- Lunar silver may be “purified” outsider gold—still exotic, but re-keyed to Shar’s moon/shadow religious display.
- The transformation likely binds the item (and wearer) to Shar’s portfolio via material change, not just enchantment overlays.

## Open Questions

- Is lunar silver a distinct material with mechanics, or a descriptive name for an alloy?
- Can it be forged deliberately, or does it only occur via divine transmutation?
