# Outsider Gold

#lore #material #outsider-tech #crafting

![Import motif](./Imports_motif.png)

## Summary

“Outsider gold” is a non-standard gold-like material repeatedly encountered in under-tunnel artifacts and weapons. It is described as having an “outsider vibe,” sometimes with a green hue, and is commonly paired with **permanent mending** enchantments.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- Many weapons/armor used by tainted gnomes and heretic Abeils were made of outsider gold.
- The “curious golden tiara” later transformed into [[Shadow's Weave]] was originally cast from outsider gold.
- Outsider gold construction often appears *cast around* gems rather than fitted after the fact.

## Observations (from notes)

- **Hue**: “green hue about it, but is not tarnished.”
- **Enchantment**: “permanent mending enchantment” appears as a recurring property.
- **Origin speculation (in-world)**: a Harper suggested its forging looked like it may have been done in **0 gravity** (**[To verify]** whether this was expert inference or factional lore).

## Why it matters

Outsider gold is one of the campaign’s clearest “artifact tells”: it flags objects as *not of local manufacture* and often correlates with outsider influence, planar contamination, and factional secrecy.

## Open Questions

- Is outsider gold a *metal*, a *plating*, or an alchemical composite?
- Who manufactures it (spelljammers, “outsiders,” cults, or something else)?
- Does it carry infection/taint intrinsically, or is it merely a convenient substrate for it?
