## Summary

Tamarand is an authority figure contacted by Cromash regarding the presence of [[Glasya]] “under Palischuk.” The response suggests Tamarand already had contact with Glasya and chose to treat the situation as controlled.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- Cromash sent: “someone cursed with geas by tiamat has summoned a devil princess under palaschuk, please help…”
- Tamarand replied (paraphrased): “All is well. She has already been in contact. She will contact you.”
- Notes characterize this as Tamarand “ratting” Cromash out to Glasya.
