## DM Sheet — Zeppo (Level 12 Dragon Monk)

Use this as a fast “drop-in” build if Zeppo needs to appear as a combatant. This is written as **Monk 12 (Way of the Ascendant Dragon)**.

**[To verify]** If Zeppo’s actual stats/equipment differ at your table, swap numbers but keep the feature list.

### Stat Block (bounded)

*Medium humanoid, unaligned (or as appropriate)*

**AC** 18 (Unarmored Defense)  
**HP** 90 (12d8 + 36)  
**Speed** 50 ft. (Unarmored Movement)  
**Proficiency Bonus** +4  
**Ki Points** 12  
**Martial Arts Die** d8  
**Initiative** +5

STR 10 (+0) DEX 20 (+5) CON 16 (+3) INT 12 (+1) WIS 16 (+3) CHA 14 (+2)

**Saving Throws** Str +4, Dex +9 (if you want “monk-y” saves, give proficiency to Dex/Str), Con +3, Int +1, Wis +3, Cha +2  
**Skills (suggested)** Acrobatics +9, Athletics +4, Insight +7, Perception +7, Stealth +9

### Core Monk Features (quick)

- **Extra Attack** (2 attacks on Attack action)
- **Flurry of Blows** (1 ki; 2 unarmed strikes as bonus action)
- **Patient Defense / Step of the Wind** (1 ki)
- **Stunning Strike** (1 ki; Con save **DC 15** on hit)
- **Deflect Missiles**, **Slow Fall**, **Evasion**, **Stillness of Mind**, **Purity of Body**
- **Unarmed Strikes count as magical**

### Ascendant Dragon Features (quick)

Pick Zeppo’s **Draconic Ancestry** damage type: **acid / cold / fire / lightning / poison**.

- **Draconic Disciple.** Draconic language; intimidation/persuasion via “dragon presence” vibe.
- **Breath of the Dragon** (2 ki; replaces one attack): 20-ft cone or 30-ft line; Dex save **DC 15**; damage = **3d8** of ancestry type (half on save).
- **Wings Unfurled** (reaction; 1 ki): after using Step of the Wind, gain a flying speed equal to walking speed until end of turn.
- **Aspect of the Wyrm** (3 ki; bonus action; 1 minute; 10-ft aura):
  - **Frightful Presence:** creatures of your choice in aura make Wis save **DC 15** or frightened until start of your next turn (repeat when they end turn in aura).
  - **Resistance:** you and allies in aura gain resistance to Zeppo’s ancestry damage type.

### Attacks

- **Unarmed Strike.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* **1d8 + 5** bludgeoning.
- **Quarterstaff (optional).** +9 to hit; *Hit:* **1d8 + 5** bludgeoning (versatile 1d10+5 if two-handed).

### Turn Script (fast)

- **Opener:** Step of the Wind to reposition → **Stunning Strike** fishing (1–2 ki) on a priority target.
- **Damage turn:** Attack (2) + Flurry (2) for 4 hits total.
- **Control turn:** **Aspect of the Wyrm (Frightful)** if you need the arena to “tilt” for drama.
- **Spectacle:** **Breath of the Dragon** when clustered targets appear (or when the crowd demands a “big move”).

### Notes (table management)

- If Zeppo is fighting PCs, keep it **nonlethal** unless the table explicitly wants lethal stakes.
- If you need him tougher without rewriting: add **+20 HP** and give him 1 extra ki “for the scene.”
