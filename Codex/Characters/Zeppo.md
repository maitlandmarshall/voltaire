## Summary

Zeppo is a recurring name tied to infernal messaging, a hostile quasit incident, and Glasya’s correspondence. The notes imply Zeppo exists as a person/entity with access to sudden appearances and to Glasya’s seal.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- A quasit attacked Cromash, claiming it had been sent by “Zeppo” (see [[Quasit (Zeppo’s Claim)]]).
- During the Glasya arc, “Zeppo” appears beside Dagoth with a letter bearing Glasya’s sigil (per notes dated **2025-03-29**).
- Glasya’s “everything is fine” back-and-forth included mention of contracts involving “Zeppo” (**[To verify]** whether Zeppo is an individual or a group name).
