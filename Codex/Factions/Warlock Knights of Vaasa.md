# Warlock Knights of Vaasa

## Summary

The Warlock Knights of Vaasa are a powerful arcane order operating in and around [[Palischuk]]. They function as a security apparatus, political authority, and trade gatekeeper—often treating planar threats with unsettling nonchalance, possibly due to **contracts** and long-term strategy.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The Warlock Knights:
  - Hold meetings with influential merchants and manage city-level affairs.
  - React strongly to outsider taint when it is placed in front of them (e.g., dissecting tainted gnome corpses).
  - Granted the party a **[[Warlock's Stone]]** (paired; sending + teleportation anchor).
- Their posture toward [[Glasya]] suggests pre-existing agreements or institutional hubris (**[To verify]** which).

## Notable Actions (from notes)

- When the party barged into an ongoing meeting, the Knights claimed they didn’t know who the party were, then demanded explanations once the merchant left.
- One Knight physically tore into a tainted corpse; it erupted into maggots (outsider-taint sign).
- A Knight involved in city trade became visibly perturbed at the idea of the [[Abeil Hive City]]’s army and its proximity.

## Entanglements

- **Tiamat water bargain**: The Knights appear to have struck a bargain with Tiamat cultists to secure Palischuk’s water supply (see [[Tamerac]]).
- **Infernal contracts**: Notes imply [[Glasya]] has contracts with the Knights (and others), and refused to produce them when asked (**[To verify]** full list, terms, and whether “Warlock Knights” is an umbrella or a specific subgroup).
