## Summary

The Harpers are an information-driven faction encountered by the party in [[Palischuk]], represented (in notes) by a high-ranking High Elf who treated information as currency and flagged the party as being surveilled by greater powers.

## What the Party Knows (in-play)

- The party met a Harper contact through Yennefer’s criminal pipeline while attempting to fence an ornate chest.
- The Harper:
  - Offered **5,000 gp** for an ornate moonstone chest, but repeatedly prioritized *information exchange*.
  - Recognized a tiny Harper symbol on an elven bone puzzle box, explaining such boxes are used to test prospective Harpers (see [[Harper Puzzle Box]]).
  - Commented that forging **[[Outsider Gold]]** looked like it may have occurred in **0-gravity** (suggesting spelljammer/void provenance).
  - Identified Tamrenac’s eye socket as resembling a portal the **Knights of Myth Drannor** closed ~130 years ago, and recommended sanctification or “address” redirection methods (**[To verify]** exact recommendation list).
  - Was alarmed by how much the party’s actions seem observed/pressured by greater powers.
